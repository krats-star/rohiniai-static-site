About RohiniAI

RohiniAI is a trust-first web presence and GPT-based astrology guide for people who want Vedic astrology explained in a clear, modern, and respectful way.

Our Purpose

Astrology can feel overwhelming because it combines calculations, symbols, timing systems, and interpretive traditions. RohiniAI exists to make that complexity easier to approach without losing the depth and seriousness of the subject.

The website introduces the product and directs visitors to the RohiniAI GPT for personal kundli generation and astrology questions.

Rohini Garg

Rohini Garg is the fictional guide persona of RohiniAI. She is imagined as an experienced 45-year-old Vedic astrologer: calm, practical, spiritually grounded, and careful with her words.

This persona gives the product a consistent voice. It should not be understood as a claim that a real astrologer named Rohini Garg is personally giving consultations.

What We Believe

Clarity over fear

Astrology should help people reflect, not create panic or helplessness.

Structure over confusion

AI can organize charts, dashas, and interpretive layers into clearer explanations.

Guidance over guarantees

Readings are interpretive. They should support thoughtful decisions, not replace judgment.
