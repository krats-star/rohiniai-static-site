# RohiniAI Website Style Guidelines V2

These guidelines are inspired by stronger astrology/product websites at a structural level only. Do not copy another site's colors, fonts, wording, graphics, layout, or brand language.

## Design Direction

RohiniAI should feel:

- premium but not heavy
- devotional but modern
- trustworthy before persuasive
- AI-forward without becoming technical
- warm enough for personal questions
- visually rich, but not crowded

## What Makes the Reference Feel Better

Use these as abstract principles:

- Clear hero promise with immediate CTA.
- Repeated CTA opportunities after major sections.
- Service-style cards that make the offering easy to scan.
- Trust cues near the top, not hidden in footer text.
- Alternating section backgrounds to create movement.
- Strong visual anchors instead of long plain text blocks.
- A productized journey: visitor knows exactly what to do next.

## RohiniAI Visual System

Palette:

- Cosmic Navy: `#071020`
- Midnight Blue: `#101A33`
- Warm Ivory: `#FFF4D8`
- Royal Gold: `#D9A227`
- Highlight Gold: `#FFD766`
- Saffron: `#F6A935`
- Deep Maroon: `#6E0000`
- Peacock Teal: `#1E6F70`

Typography:

- Display: Cormorant Garamond
- Body/UI: Inter
- Avoid overly decorative body fonts.

Graphics:

- Use approved Rohini assets sparingly: lotus, diya, zodiac arc, gold ornaments.
- Use one main visual anchor per section.
- Avoid filling all blank space with decoration.

Layout:

- Hero: full-bleed image with strong readable overlay.
- Use cards for scan-friendly offerings and FAQ.
- Use compact trust strips for credibility.
- Avoid nested cards.
- Keep primary CTA visible in hero, mid-page, and final CTA.

Messaging:

- Do not overclaim accuracy.
- Do not imply a real human astrologer if using Rohini Garg.
- Emphasize: structure, privacy-aware flow, accessibility, free-to-start GPT access, and clear explanations.
