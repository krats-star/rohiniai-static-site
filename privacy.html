Privacy

Privacy Policy

This first version of the RohiniAI website is static. It does not collect birth details, create accounts, or run kundli calculations on the website.

Information This Website Collects

This static website is designed as a product introduction and GPT funnel. In its current form, it does not ask visitors to submit birth date, birth time, birth place, phone number, or payment information.

Use of ChatGPT

When visitors click the RohiniAI GPT link, they leave this website and use ChatGPT. Personal questions, birth details, and chat history are handled inside the user's ChatGPT account and are subject to OpenAI's privacy controls and account settings.

Visitors should share only the information they are comfortable providing in ChatGPT.

Analytics and Hosting

When this site is published, the hosting provider may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser, device, pages visited, and timestamps for security, performance, and analytics. Any analytics should be configured with privacy in mind.

Future Features

If RohiniAI later adds forms, newsletters, login, payments, or website-based kundli tools, this policy should be updated before those features go live.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact details provided on the Contact page.
